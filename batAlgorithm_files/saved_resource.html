Bat Algorithm-History and main Idea​

  • ​

  • Meta-Heuristic algorithms are becoming powerful methods to solve tough optimization problems. ​

  • Bat Algorithm is a bio-inspired algorithm developed by Yang in 2010.​

  • Uses frequency-tuning technique to increase the diversity of solutions in the population.​

  • Uses automatic zooming to try to balance exploration and exploitation during search process.​

  • Mimics variations of pulse emission rates and loudness of bats when searching for prey.​
